Donations are accepted from 8 a.m.-8 p.m. (Monday-Sunday). Visit their website for more information on how to deposit cash donations.

Smart: 10, 25, 50, 100, 300, 500 or 1000

The Philippine Red Cross - National Headquarters in Manila is accepting in-kind donations. They could also arrange for donation pick-ups. Visit their website for more information on how to deposit cash donations.
